Cloudiness

These charts show a weak correlation between latitude and regional cloudiness. Interestingly, in the northern hemisphere, while the correlation is weak, cloudiness increases further from the equator. In the southern hemisphere, the correlation is a little stronger but it is inverse. In the southern hemisphere cloudiness decreases further from the equator.
